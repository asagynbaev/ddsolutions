Who We Are

We are a group of design driven individuals passionate about creating innovations.

D&D Solutions – is a multidisciplinary engineering company, uniting some main directions of activity, one of which is a design of hydraulic and hydropower projects, water supply projects, water disposal projects and also flat control of territories.

Within this activity we fulfill a complete range of design works, beginning from the creation of the project conception, its economic efficiency up to the moment of its commissioning.

Experienced and competent design team, having a vast practice on international projects fulfills works with a high quality and safety.

Professional personnel

Owing to the experience and practice of our engineers, we can guarantee a high quality of our projects. Our specialists have a great experience in development of international projects. Some of them are members of such international professional societies as ICOLD.

Optimal project solutions

Each object, if it is needed, passes all steps of service life cycle, beginning the concept development (Pre-Feasibility Study) and its approval in international financial organizations and up to commissioning of the project.

Projecting advanced technologies

Advanced software is used in design of objects. The project can be made according to BIM technology at the request of a customer. The company applies into design process technologies of artificial intelligence.

Terms of projection and safety

We always try to fulfill works exactly in time and try to develop optimal design solutions for the reduction of expenses and implementation time, without compromise for the project safety.
